LC4

ElsieFour ("LC4") was created by Alan Kaminsky. It is a low-tech cipher, which means that it is meant to be easily computable by hand. LC4 is intended to encrypt English sentences and thus consists only of characters and can not encrypt individual bits.

Quick info

alphabet:
#_23456789abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz

key:
A random permutation of the LC4-alphabet

nonce:
Character sequence to randomize ciphertext; must be 6 characters or more

signature:
Signature that proves authenticity of the message

header data:
Additional authentication;
